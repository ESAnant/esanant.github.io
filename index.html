About Me

I am a passionate hardware design engineer with expertise in digital logic design, embedded systems, and semiconductor technologies. My journey in engineering is driven by a fascination with the intricate world of silicon and the endless possibilities it offers for innovation.

With a strong foundation in Verilog, SystemVerilog, and various EDA tools, I specialize in creating efficient, scalable hardware solutions. My work spans from low-level RTL design to high-level system architecture, always with a focus on performance optimization and power efficiency.
