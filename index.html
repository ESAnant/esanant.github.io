01. About Me

My journey into the world of electronics began not in a lab, but with a simple curiosity for the devices around me. Dismantling old gadgets as a teenager sparked a passion that led me through a B.Tech in Electronics and a specialized M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the National University of Singapore.

This path has taken me from foundational principles of digital logic to advanced topics in VLSI design, memory technologies, and hardware acceleration for neural networks. My experience spans both academic research and industry-focused internships, where I've designed and verified components like AHB-to-APB bridges and optimized interconnects at the 45nm technology node.

I am driven by the challenge of bridging the gap between theoretical models and tangible, high-performance hardware. My goal is to contribute to the next generation of computing systems by designing smarter, faster, and more efficient integrated circuits.

Core Competencies:

  • Verilog & SystemVerilog
  • VLSI & IC Design
  • FPGA Prototyping (Xilinx)
  • Hardware Acceleration
  • In-Memory Computing
  • Semiconductor Device Physics
  • RTL Design & Verification
  • Cadence & Synopsys Tools

02. Featured Projects

Featured Project

FPGA Neural Network Accelerator

Developed a hardware accelerator for MLP neural network inference on a Xilinx Zynq-7000 FPGA. Achieved significant performance gains by implementing optimized pipelining and loop unrolling strategies in Verilog and HLS.

  • Verilog
  • HLS
  • Xilinx Vivado
  • Pipelining
Featured Project

In-Memory Compute Circuit Design

Designed and simulated novel in-memory computing circuits aimed at accelerating neural network operations. Utilized NeuroSim and PyTorch for modeling and analysis, focusing on quantization optimization to enhance efficiency.

  • Circuit Design
  • NeuroSim
  • PyTorch
  • MuMax3
Industry Internship

AHB to APB Bridge

Designed a modular and robust AHB to APB bridge during an internship at Maven Silicon. Focused on creating a seamless bus protocol translation with efficient RTL implementation and comprehensive verification using SystemVerilog.

  • Verilog HDL
  • SystemVerilog
  • RTL Verification
  • AMBA Bus
